Teaching is our focus

We believe instructors are the most important link in the chain.

Coursefork is a web tool that lets them create, find, and collaborate on open courses.

We believe every coder is a potential teacher. Collaborative course design will open up the joy of teaching to a new audience and increase the relevance of course content for everyone.

This is a new perspective

Many education courses focus on creating and delivering educational media to students.

These are important businesses, but they’re not enough.

We believe that prepared teachers are the most important part of teaching. We are a company by teachers, for teachers, dedicated to helping our users design the world’s best courses.

Forking will change everything

Forking means:

  • Everyone can have their own copy of anything
  • Anyone can modify, customize and improve
  • Collaboration is distributed and easy
